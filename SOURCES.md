## Research Notes

### Historical Context

Strigoi beliefs must be understood in historical context:
- Pre-germ theory disease explanation
- Limited understanding of decomposition
- Religious and social control mechanisms
- Genuine fear and community responses
- Real historical practices (exhumations, stakings)

### Cultural Respect

When studying Strigoi:
- Recognize as serious cultural tradition
- Avoid sensationalization or mockery
- Respect Romanian cultural heritage
- Acknowledge real historical impact on communities
- Understand functional role in society

### Modern Academic Approaches

Contemporary research focuses on:
- Historical documentation and analysis
- Sociological and anthropological perspectives
- Medical/forensic explanations
- Cultural heritage preservation
- Popular culture influence studies
